* Procedures
** Cleaning Glass
   Rinse with EthylAcetate -> Acetone
   Put in (cleaned) shotglass filled with acetone
   Sonicate for 6 minutes
   Blow dry thoroughly
   Asher for 5 min at 200 W
   Clean with water
   Blow dry thoroughly
   Dry in oven
